Password Generator
This program is a password generator.
You can choose what password do you want with letter e(easy), m(middle), h(hard)
You need to write what length password do you want
Hard passwords have a mix of lowercase letters, uppercase letters, numbers, symbols and length between 8 to 50.
Middle passwords have a mix of lowercase and uppercase letters, numbers, symbols and length between 6 to 40.
Easy passwords have a mix of lowercase letters, numbers and length between 4 to 30.
The passwords should be random, generating a new password every time the user asks for a new password.

Attention
When password is generating it's copied to your clipboard
You can exit when you want
just type exit or ctr+c
